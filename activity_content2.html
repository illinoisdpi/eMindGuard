DPI: Research Poster Presentation

Research Culture event

We had the privilege of presenting our research poster on the eMindGuard app at the Discovery Partners Institute (DPI) Research Poster Presentation event. The poster, which highlights innovative approaches in mental health assessment through our eMindGuard app, drew significant attention from attendees. During the event, we had the honor of meeting remarkable individuals, including Nobel Peace Prize laureate Dr. Donald J Wuebbles. Our discussions with him and other like-minded researchers led to the exchange of valuable ideas and sparked new collaboration opportunities, which we are excited to pursue in the coming months. The event was an enriching experience, and we are thrilled to have shared our work with such a distinguished audience.

Date and Venue

  • Date: 08-27-2024
  • Venue: Chicago, IL, US
