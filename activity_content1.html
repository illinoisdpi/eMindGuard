Cardiff Health Research: Successful Together programme

Research Culture event

We are pleased to announce our participation in the "Research Culture" event titled Cardiff Health Research: Successful Together Programme, organized by Dr. Jessica Baillie from Cardiff University. This event was held at the University Hospital of Wales and featured a variety of insightful activities and presentations.

Date and Venue

  • Date: 07-11-2024
  • Venue:Cardiff, Wales, UK

The eMindGuard app poster presented at the Cardiff Health Research event, showcasing the integration of social media, environmental, and locational data for precision mental health assessment. This innovative approach aims to provide a more nuanced and accurate evaluation of mental health vulnerability, leveraging cutting-edge technologies.

Dr. Diana Contreras, our UK-based team member, presenting the eMindGuard app poster at the Cardiff Health Research event. Her presentation highlighted the app's potential to revolutionize mental health assessments through advanced data integration and AI-driven insights.
