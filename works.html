About eMindGuard

Innovative Mental Health Assessment

eMindGuard is an innovative app designed to provide a comprehensive evaluation of mental health vulnerability, especially for young adults including college students. It uniquely integrates social media data, environmental factors, and geospatial information, offering a nuanced understanding of individual mental health.

Holistic Approach

The app stands out by considering not just user's social media sentiments but also environmental data (like climate and air quality) and locational data (proximity to various facilities and events). This holistic approach provides a more accurate and personalized assessment of mental health vulnerabilities.

Explainable AI and Machine Learning

eMindGuard utilizes state-of-the-art explainable AI techniques and machine learning models for a predictive and precise assessment of mental health vulnerabilities. This ensures transparency and aids users in understanding their mental health better.

Support and Tailored Recommendations

The app not only offers profound insights into mental well-being but also provides personalized recommendations for improving mental health. It includes a feature to notify trusted family or friends in case of a critical threshold in mental health vulnerability, fostering a supportive community.
